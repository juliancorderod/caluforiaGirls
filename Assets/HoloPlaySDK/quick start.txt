HoloPlay SDK Quick Start guide:

- Right click in your hierarchy and click "HoloPlay -> HoloPlay Capture"

or

- Drag the "HoloPlay Capture" prefab into your scene!

The inspector for the HoloPlay is where most of the elements can be customized. 
Play around with settings to achieve desired results.
